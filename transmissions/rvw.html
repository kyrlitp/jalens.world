time capsule: june 29, 2022

written in prague, uploaded to jalens.world — july 2025
roe v. wade had just fallen. i was 23. i thought maybe i was being dramatic. i wasn’t.
this is part of the archive — a signal from before it broke.

we’re in really tough times. here’s why it is important, now more than ever, that you create.

ROE V WADE WAS JUST OVERTURNED.

THINGS ARE LOOKING HORRIBLE FOR THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA. I GIVE THE COUNTRY 3 MORE YEARS, BUT THAT’S JUST ME.

IT FEELS LIKE WE SHOULDN’T BE CREATING. IT’S A “DISTRACTION”.

BUT IT’S REALLY IMPORTANT THAT WE DO.

I WANT TO DISPEL THE IDEA THAT OUR ART IS SOMEHOW GOING TO “CHANGE” THE WORLD, AND HOW THINGS ARE.

THAT MAY BE WHAT THE HIGHEST PROFILE ARTISTS HAVE THE ABILITY TO DO, BUT NOT US.

INSTEAD, WE HAVE A MORE IMPORTANT ROLE ARGUABLY —

TO SHOW A SNAPSHOT.

“WHO WERE THESE PEOPLE, LIVING IN 2022? WHAT WERE THEY LIKE?
WHAT WERE THEIR HOPES?
DREAMS?
DESIRES?
WHAT WERE THEY THINKING?
DID THEY SOUND LIKE ME?
DID THEY ACT LIKE ME?
HOW AM I DIFFERENT FROM THEM?
AM I DIFFERENT FROM THEM?
COULD WHAT HAPPENED TO THEM
HAPPEN TO ME?”

IT’S ONE THING TO READ ABOUT YOUR ANCESTORS IN A HISTORY BOOK.

IT’S ANOTHER TO HEAR THEIR VOICES.
SEE THEIR CLOTHES.
WATCH VIDEOS OF THEM.

THE ANCESTOR JUMPS OFF THE PAGE AND SMILES OR CRIES.

THE OBSERVER LEARNS SOMETHING FROM THEIR REACTION.

THIS TOO SHALL PASS, AND SO THE ART WE CREATE TODAY TELLS THE STORY OF WHAT IS/WAS/WILL HAPPEN IN THIS MOMENT.

AND THAT’S WORTH SOMETHING.
